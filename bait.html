Baitwatch: Episode 23, Controlled Opposition

DIS, Online, 2019
Collaboration with Ed Fornieles


Baitwatch imagined a Pixar-style educational series devoted to exploring media vulnerabilities via a loveable worm-on-the-hook named Bait.

It was first exhibited at the Baltimore Museum of Art, part of DIS | A Good Crisis. A separate museum screening was followed by a conversation between myself and Lisa Snowden McCray, editor-in-chief of Baltimore Beat.
